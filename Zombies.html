Know your Enemy..

"To know your Enemy you must become your Enemy" -Sun Tzu-The Art of War

Wise words from The Art of War author and philosopher Sun Tzu and credit to him for passing along this and many other fine philosophical doctrines from the 5th century BC. Even today, over 2 Millenia in the future much of the information he passed on to us is still reverent and useful in survival and warfare.

Infected

Infected zombies are bad news. Full Stop. They can be fast or slow, roam in groups or alone. Victims can hold on to various amounts of hosts' memory. A cure is possable.

Unknowns

How'd that happen? There on the streets, Punching out of graves and coming through your window. Very dangerous as they are too unknown. They are powerful as a group but easy to fight alone.

Animals

Man's best friends are not always man's best friends, They can outrun you and are sneaky as hell. The pack mentally is strong with this zombie group which makes up for their general lack of intelligence.

Supernatural/Magic

Someone made Harry mad! okay, The rarest zombie types by far. A result of witches, Sorcerers and Voodoo practitioners. They follow the masters' orders..Kill the master or seek a helpful wizard.
