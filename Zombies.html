Know your Enemy..

"To know your Enemy you must become your Enemy" -Sun Tzu-The Art of War

Wise words from The Art of War author and philosopher Sun Tzu and credit to him for passing along this and many other fine philosophical doctrines from the 5th century BC. Even today, over 2 Millenia in the future much of the information he passed on to us is still reverent and useful in survival and warfare.

I think we would all agree Sun Tzu could have never foreseen a zombie apocalypse, Many rarely do, If he did he might have said something along the lines of "To become your Enemy is to kill, infect and eat your friend's brain!"

How to fight..

Seven simple rules to follow I think we can all agree on that, but how do we effectively fight the walking dead if we can't escape or hide?

The surefire way to remove a zombie threat permanently is the destruction of the brain. Of course, you could chop off its Legs, Arms and head but it will probably wriggle around your ankles like a naughty snake. Chopping off its head may lead to an evil biting blowing ball rolling around the place...Not good!

The skull is tough, and thick and has evolved to deflect and absorb hits to protect the brain helpful for us but not when it comes to fighting zombies. You have to use the appropriate weapons when dealing with this fact as punching a zombie in the skull will most likely result in a broken wrist and a bitten hand. So use a real weapon if you are able like an axe or hammer when fighting face to face.
